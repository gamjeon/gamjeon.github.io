Ask Your Question

Who are we?
We are a bimetal thermometer manufacturer from Jiangmen, Guangdong, China.
What is our main business?
We sell bimetal thermometers and also sell bimetal thermometer parts. We have more than 10 years of production experience and are a reliable supplier for Chinese customers. (In fact, because bimetal thermometer is not so generally popular in China, our products are exported to foreign countries through Chinese customers, so we hope to communicate with foreign customers straight-to-straight.)
Whether to accept OEM production?
We accept OEM/ODM service. Generally speaking, customers can directly request to engrave the brand or add a product logo. But for a private version with a novel shape or internal structure, a certain mold opening cost is required. We have conducted in-depth research in this field for many years and have extensive industry contacts in the Pearl River Delta of China, which can help you develop at a lower cost and produce your own products.
Whether to support sample delivery?
Of course, if you need a sample of the thermometer, you only need to pay the corresponding postage, and we will deliver the product to you. Of course this only supports the public version of the thermometer.
What is the capacity of our factory?
Usually can produce 150,000-200,000 bimetal thermometers per month.

Bimetal thermometer manufacturer from china

We are a bimetal thermometer manufacturer from Jiangmen, Guangdong, China. We specialize in the production of thermometers and thermometer parts, with a history of more than ten years. Our products include BBQ thermometers, grill thermometers, pot lid thermometers, kettle lid thermometers and so on.
